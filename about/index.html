About Me

Hi! I'm Daniel, a graduate student in Electrical Engineering at Stanford University.  My primary research interest is applying machine learning to pediatric healthcare.  I work with other researchers and healthcare providers, using mathematical analyses and optimizations to improve the quality of patient care.

My favorite hobby is rock climbing, both indoors and outdoors.  I collect maps from my favorite national and state parks and monuments, and hope to one day visit them all.  I also enjoy custom-building computers for a variety of uses, including scientific computing and gaming.
